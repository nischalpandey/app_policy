Privacy Policy

At Hamro Rent, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard the information you provide to us through our Rental Service App.

Information We Collect

When you use Hamro Rent, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • IMEI (International Mobile Equipment Identity)
  • Other general information such as your location, preferences, and usage patterns

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and personalize our rental services
  • To communicate with you and respond to your inquiries
  • To improve our app's functionality and user experience
  • To send you relevant updates, promotions, and offers
  • To prevent fraud, unauthorized access, or any illegal activities
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights and interests

Data Security

At Hamro Rent, we take the security of your information seriously. We implement industry-standard measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Sharing of Information

We may share your information with trusted third parties only for the purposes mentioned in this Privacy Policy. These parties include:

  • Service providers who assist us in delivering our rental services (e.g., payment processors, customer support)
  • Law enforcement agencies or other authorized entities when required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • Third parties with your consent or at your direction

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify or update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on our app. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
